My Story

I started in neuroscience research, fascinated by how people make decisions and what drives them to act. My work focused on reward psychology – essentially, what makes our brains tick when we're choosing what matters to us.

Today, I bring that scientific understanding to tech startup advertising, where I handle user acquisition and customer onboarding. This combination of brain science and business gives me a unique edge in high-ticket sales: I don't just understand the practical side of closing deals – I understand the psychology behind why people say yes.
